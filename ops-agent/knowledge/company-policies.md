# Company Policies

The company name is always written as "Asantico", never "Asantico LLC".
Invoices and estimates never include tenant names; work is routed to the property manager.
Client emails are kept brief and never state the dollar amount in the body.
No em dashes appear in any generated document.
A minimum charge applies to any dispatched job regardless of time on site.
Before and after job photos have GPS and EXIF metadata stripped before they are attached.
